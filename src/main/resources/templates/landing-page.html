Financial Accuracy

Depreciation That Complies with Accounting Standards

JustJava FAMS automates depreciation in line with IFRS/GAAP requirements, eliminating the manual calculation errors that distort your balance sheet.

SLM
Straight-Line Method
Spreads the cost of an asset evenly over its useful life — ideal for stable, long-lived assets with predictable usage.
RBM
Reducing Balance Method
Applies a fixed percentage to the declining book value each year — better for assets that lose value faster early on.
DDB
Double Declining Balance
Doubles the straight-line rate, front-loading depreciation. Perfect for technology assets with rapid early obsolescence.
